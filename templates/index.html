Music Recommendation System

Welcome to Spopitt! This is a music recommendation system that analyzes your Spotify data to provide you with personalized music recommendations based on your listening habits.

Features:

  • Personalized music recommendations
  • Analysis of your Spotify data
  • User-friendly interface

How to use:

  1. Login with your Spotify account by clicking the button below.
  2. Once logged in, the system will analyze your Spotify data.
  3. Based on your listening habits, you will receive personalized music recommendations.
